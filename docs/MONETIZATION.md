# Wanderverse — Monetization Strategy

## 1. Revenue Model: Freemium SaaS

Wanderverse uses a tiered freemium model with a clear value ladder that converts users from free to paid through usage limits and feature gating.

## 2. Pricing Tiers

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Target |
|------|---------|--------|--------|
| **Free** | $0 | $0 | Casual travelers, trial users |
| **Wanderer** | $9 | $89 (18% off) | Enthusiasts, 3-6 trips/year |
| **Explorer** | $19 | $189 (17% off) | Creators, influencers, families |
| **Voyager** | $49 | $489 (17% off) | Agencies, power users, brands |

## 3. Feature Gating Strategy

### Free Tier (Acquisition Hook)
- 3 active trips (archived trips don't count)
- 50 photos per trip
- 2 basic map styles
- 3 AI generations per trip
- Public & private sharing
- **Goal**: Let users experience the "magic moment" (first immersive story) without paying

### Wanderer ($9/mo) — The Conversion Tier
- Unlimited trips
- 500 photos per trip
- 8 map styles
- 10 AI generations per trip
- 2 collaborators
- 50 AI photo enhancements/month
- **Goal**: Capture users who hit the free limits after 1-2 trips

### Explorer ($19/mo) — The Power User Tier
- Unlimited trips & photos
- All map styles + custom
- Unlimited AI generations
- 10 collaborators
- Custom domains
- Advanced analytics
- API access
- **Goal**: For serious storytellers and content creators

### Voyager ($49/mo) — The Business Tier
- Everything in Explorer
- Unlimited collaborators
- White-label exports (remove Wanderverse branding)
- Dedicated support
- Priority AI queue
- Volume discounts on AI
- **Goal**: Travel agencies, brands, tourism boards

## 4. Expansion Revenue

### Usage-Based Add-ons
- **Extra Storage**: $5/10GB/month beyond quota
- **AI Overage**: $0.50/100 AI credits beyond plan
- **Video Processing**: $2/video for MP4 export
- **Priority Processing**: $5/month for faster AI queues

### One-Time Purchases
- **Premium Templates**: $5-15 per template pack
- **Story Export Credits**: $3/PDF, $5/static site, $10/MP4
- **Gift Subscriptions**: 3-month or 12-month gift cards

### B2B Revenue (Future)
- **Travel Agency White-label**: $199/month per agency
- **Tourism Board Partnerships**: Custom pricing
- **API Access for Partners**: $0.01/request beyond included quota

## 5. Conversion Funnel

```
Sign Up (100%) 
  → Create First Trip (60%)
    → Upload Photos (45%)
      → Generate AI Story (30%)
        → Publish/Share (20%)
          → Hit Free Limit (15%)
            → Upgrade to Paid (4-6%)
```

### Conversion Triggers
- **Soft Limit**: Warning at 80% of quota with CTA
- **Hard Limit**: Blocked action with upgrade modal
- **Feature Tease**: Show paid features with "Upgrade to unlock" badge
- **Success Celebration**: After publishing first story, suggest upgrade for more

## 6. Pricing Psychology

- **Anchoring**: $49 Voyager makes $19 Explorer feel reasonable
- **Decoy Effect**: $9 Wanderer positioned as "most popular"
- **Annual Bias**: 17-18% annual discount encourages commitment
- **Social Proof**: "Join 47,000+ travelers" on pricing page
- **Loss Aversion**: "You're 3 photos away from your limit"
